OTP Scam Awareness

OTP scams occur when fraudsters trick users into sharing one-time passwords used for secure verification.

How OTP Scams Happen

Scammers pretend to be bank officials or customer support agents.

Golden Rule

No company, bank, or government office will ever ask for your OTP.

Kavalkaran Mission

Kavalkaran spreads OTP awareness to protect common people.
