Cyber Safety Tips for Students

Students are frequent internet users and are often targeted by cyber criminals. Cyber safety awareness is essential.

Common Online Risks

  • Fake internships
  • Gaming scams
  • Social media fraud

Basic Safety Rules

Use strong passwords, avoid sharing personal information, and verify links.
