Online Job Scams in India

Online job scams are increasing rapidly in India. Students, freshers, and job seekers are the main targets of cyber criminals.

How Fake Job Scams Work

Scammers contact victims through WhatsApp, Telegram, or email pretending to be HR from well-known companies.

Common Warning Signs

  • Asking money for registration
  • No official company email
  • Guaranteed job offers
