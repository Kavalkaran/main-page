Social Media Scams in India

Social media platforms like Instagram, Facebook, and WhatsApp are widely misused by scammers.

Common Social Media Scams

  • Fake giveaways
  • Impersonation profiles
  • Malicious links

Safety Tips

Always verify profiles and never click suspicious links.
