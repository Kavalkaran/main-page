What Is Phishing Scam?

Phishing is a cyber attack where fake messages or websites trick users into sharing passwords, OTPs, or bank details.

Types of Phishing

  • SMS phishing
  • Email phishing
  • Fake websites

How to Stay Safe

Never click unknown links and always verify the website address carefully.
