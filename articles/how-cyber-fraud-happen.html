How Cyber Frauds Happen in India

Published by Kavalkaran • Updated 2026

Cyber frauds are increasing rapidly in India. From fake calls to online scams, millions of people are affected every year. Understanding how these frauds work is the first step to staying safe.

Common Types of Cyber Frauds

1. Fake Customer Care Calls

Fraudsters pretend to be bank or company representatives and trick users into sharing OTPs or card details.

2. Phishing Messages

Messages with fake links claiming rewards or urgent account issues are designed to steal personal information.

3. UPI & QR Code Scams

Attackers misuse QR codes and fake payment requests to steal money instantly.
